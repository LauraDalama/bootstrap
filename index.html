WELCOME TO THE HOME OF CONTEMPORARY ART & DESIGN

Priveekollektie Contemporary Art | Design is a leading international gallery of contemporary and functional art based in the Netherlands. Opened in 2006 by avid Dutch collectors Irving and Miriam van Dijk, the gallery utilizes the founders personal approach, expansive knowledge, eye for detail, and unique taste to merge together the two disciplines, art and design. Centralized in Europe, their exceptional exhibitions annually participate in internationally renowned fairs in Paris, Basel, Miami, London, and New York.
